The heart behind the weekend

The undoing of what
has become tangled.

The Unraveling is not a women's conference about women. It is a gathering around the Lord — Father, Son, and Spirit — from which everything else flows.

We believe that wholeness and healing for the feminine soul come through deeper communion with God. That abiding produces overflowing. That those who look upon the Lord are radiant.

"Those who look to him are radiant, and their faces shall never be ashamed." — Psalm 34:5
